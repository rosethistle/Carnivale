Carnivale

A Masquarade Dance

An evening of music, dancing, and sheer elegance.

We are hosting this wonderful event to help support our vibrant artistic community here in Ladysmith. Come join us!

Details

  • When

    On October 28th, 2023. Doors open at 7pm and close at midnight. The organizers turn into pumpkins then!

  • Where

    We will be hosting this soiree at Aggie Hall. 1110, 1st Ave, Ladysmith, BC.

  • Tickets

    Tickets are $50. This helps us make sure youll have an evening to remeber! From music, food, drink and merryment it will be quite the Shin-Dig! All procedes will go to the Ladysmith Arts Council to help us find our new location. Click to buy your tickets through the Ladysmith Arts Council website.

  • Music

    We are very lucky to say that the Soul Shakers will be providing us with an evening of live music. Heres a sample of their music below!

  • Food

    There will be a variety of finger food available through the evening. They will all be labeled and made by local chefs to bring the delicate and sophosticated tastes to this evenings event.

  • Age restrictions

    This is an event that will have liquor on the premeses, that means that although we can offer winde, beer and other lovely drinks to help your evening be memorable, that means that no-one under the age of 19 is allowed at thjis event.

  • Dress Code

    Dress to the Nines! This is one of those rare occasions that you are encouraged to dress as nice as you want! Such a rartiy in our day and age. I know there will be more than a few ballgowns at the event.

  • Masks

    Masks Required! Although not the ones were used to! as this is a masquarade dance, we hope everyone comes ready to enjoy as night of mystery and intrigue as we don our creative and artistic face coverings. We will have a few events leading up to the dance that will be some workshops on how to make your own mask!
